The why

Africa makes the sound.
Africa should own it.

For too long our artists have been squeezed — by piracy sites that leak music before release and pay nothing, and by faraway platforms that take the lion’s share. DeeMusiq flips the model. Artists publish on their own accounts, decide what their work is worth, and draw their royalties when they choose.

  • ⬡ Fair compensation for every stream & download
  • ◆ Artists keep their masters and their rights
  • ★ A formal platform for positive cultural exchange
  • ▼ Building 100+ jobs across the arts & IT sectors
